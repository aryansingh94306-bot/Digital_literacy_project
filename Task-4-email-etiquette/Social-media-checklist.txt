Do:

Keep Your Accounts Safe: Create strong passwords and don’t reuse them.
Present Yourself Well: Your online activity shapes how others see you.
Pause Before Posting: A quick rethink can save you from mistakes.
Show Courtesy: Being polite online goes a long way.
Confirm Before Sharing: Make sure the information is true before passing it on.

Don’t:

Reveal Sensitive Details: Personal information should stay private.
Click Without Thinking: Be cautious with unknown or suspicious links.
Spread False Information: Avoid forwarding unverified news or rumors.
React Harshly: Stay away from rude or aggressive comments.
Post Excessively Personal Content: Keep some parts of your life offline.
